Bleach

The main character is a 15yr old kid named Ichigo Kurosaki who can see ghosts.

One day, he meets a girl named Rukia Kuchiki who tells him she is a shinigami who kills evil spirits and helps souls to the afterlife.

A hollow attacks them and Rukia is badly injured, so she gives Ichigo her shinigami powers and Ichigo kills the hollow.

Naruto

An orphan in a village called Konoha in a country called Fire Country is shunned by his village.

Within this village, there are elite humans that have exceptional abilities by utilizing their natural energies and magic and training their minds for combat.

This orphan, Naruto, aspires to be the strongest ninja in Konoha, titled as the "Hokage" so that he will be acknowledged by the village and no longer be hated.

Fairy Tail

Lucy, a 17 year old girl, sets out on journey to become a full-fledged wizard and joins the strongest and most (in) famous guild FAIRY TAIL where she meets Natsu, Happy, Gray and Erza, who treat her more like family than friends.

One Piece

One Piece is the story of Monkey D. Luffy, a young man who has a single dream: To find the legendary treasure known as the One Piece and become the King of the Pirates.

Alongside a crew of trusted friends, Luffy sails the dangerous seas of the Grand Line to find Laugh Tale, the hidden island containing the One Piece.
